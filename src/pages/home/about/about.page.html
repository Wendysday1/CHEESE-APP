About Us About Sheezy

The specialty food retail is establishing itself in a rapidly changing digital environment, and the Sheezy mobile app is spearheading the revolution in the artisanal cheese industry. Sheezy is a solution to an uncommon problem, which is the challenge of having a wide, and not inferior quality line of favorite cheeses that can be bought without resorting to the individual specialty stores or small supermarket offerings. Sheezy makes the search of the ideal curds effortless and hassle-free by introducing a global counter of cheese to the pocket of the user.

In addition to its selection, Sheezy is also good at providing an optimal mobile experience. The user interface (UI) of the application is simple, easy to use, and simple to navigate through as it allows browsing hundreds of options with a swipe of a finger, just like a photo gallery. The logistical chain is of high priority in Sheezy, which is the recognition of the fact that specialty food requires special processing. It offers speed and temperature controlled delivery, that preserves the delicate flavor and texture of all cheeses, with the gap between small batch production and the refrigerator door of the consumer.

Importantly, Sheezy is not only a marketplace as it also represents a digital cheesemonger and an educational source. All profiles of the cheeses are full of facts, such as detailed descriptions of tasting, recommended beverage and wine matches, and storage guidelines. This knowledge base combined allows users not only the purchase of the choice but also to know its history, provenance, making an amateur buyer a dedicated connoisseur. Through its ability to place consumers directly in contact with the finest artisanal production in the world and locally, Sheezy is forging the future of specialty food shopping.
